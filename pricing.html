Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after my 3 free charts?

You can continue to view and share your existing 3 charts forever. To create a 4th visualization, you can either upgrade to Premium or delete an existing chart to free up a slot.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription?

Yes, you can cancel at any time from your dashboard. You will maintain access to Premium features until the end of your current billing cycle.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption. For Premium users, we offer password protection for sensitive financial or operational dashboards.

Do you offer student or non-profit discounts?

We do! Contact our support team with proof of your status, and we'll provide a custom discount code for your Premium plan.
