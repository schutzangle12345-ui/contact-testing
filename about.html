We Kill Ugly Charts

At contact-testing, we believe that data insights shouldn't be locked behind a $50,000 BI subscription or a computer science degree.

The Problem

Modern BI tools are broken for the average person. They are either prohibitively expensive, leading to "paywall block," or so complex that non-technical users give up before seeing a single bar chart.

We saw marketers struggling to prove ROI, product managers unable to share quick cohort charts, and analysts spending hours formatting Excel sheets that nobody looked at because they were "ugly."

contact-testing was built to solve this. We provide free data visualization basics that allow you to go from a raw CSV to a stunning, interactive dashboard in under 60 seconds.

"Data is only as valuable as the decisions it inspires. We make those decisions easier by making the data clearer."

Our Core Principles

No-Code Forever

If you need to write SQL or Python to see your data, we haven't done our job. Our drag-and-drop builder is designed for anyone who can use a mouse.

Design by Default

Most people aren't designers. We provide sensible defaults and pre-built templates so your dashboards always look professional and high-contrast.

Instant Sharing

No more PDF screenshots. Share live, interactive links or embed your charts directly into Notion, Confluence, or your own website with ease.

Who We Serve

We build for the startup founder tracking their first 100 customers. We build for the agency showing campaign results to clients. We build for the student presenting a thesis.
